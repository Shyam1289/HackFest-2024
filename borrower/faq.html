Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for borrowing money? To borrow money, you need to be at least 18 years old, have a steady source of income, and provide identification.
How much money can I borrow? The amount you can borrow depends on various factors such as your income, credit history, and the lending policies of our company.
What are the interest rates? Interest rates vary based on the amount borrowed and your creditworthiness. Please contact us for specific details.
How long does it take to get approved? Approval times can vary, but in many cases, you can get approved within 24 hours.
What happens if I can't repay the loan on time? If you're unable to repay the loan on time, please contact us immediately to discuss your options. Failure to repay may result in additional fees and negatively affect your credit score.
